A Perfect Blend of Professional Services, Inspiring Ideas, and Professional Staff 2007 – An event company that combines meticulous planning with awe-inspiring design and concept…oh, and it OWNS all of the services and equipment necessary for the event itself. Mix it all together and the end result is The Perfect Event. Founded in 2007, the company was designed to be a true One Stop Shop for all of its clients. Tireless effort and sensational growth have resulted in the realization of this vision. Aside from the exceptional event planning department itself, The Perfect Event also owns and operates Security, Transportation, A/V Production, Staging, Photo & Video Services, Disc Jockey Services, and Event Rentals. Comprised of nearly 200 total employees, The Perfect Event has become a powerhouse in the industry. In 2019, the company produced and provided services for over 600 individual events consisting of weddings, festivals, corporate, collegiate, and other private parties.
